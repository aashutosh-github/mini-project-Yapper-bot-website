Here are some fun facts about AI
  1. AI's Early Beginnings: AI as a concept dates back to ancient myths, but the field truly began with Alan Turing's work in the 1950s.
  2. AI and Art: AI can create original art pieces, from painting to music, demonstrating creativity beyond logical algorithms.
  3. AI in Healthcare: AI helps diagnose diseases, sometimes even more accurately than human doctors by analyzing vast amounts of data quickly.
  4. AI Bias: AI systems can sometimes inherit biases from the data they're trained on, leading to unfair decisions. Ensuring diverse data helps mitigate this.
  5. Reinforcement Learning: This is a type of machine learning where AI learns by receiving rewards for performing desired actions, akin to training a pet.
